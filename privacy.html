Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Smart Light Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photo Library Access

Smart Light Assistant requires access to your device's camera and photo library for the following purposes:

  • Camera access: To provide real-time preview and capture photos/videos with fill light effects
  • Photo library access: To save captured photos and videos to your device's photo library
  • Photo library selection: To allow you to select existing photos or videos from your library

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • We do not access your photos or videos beyond what you explicitly select
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • We do not share any data with third parties

How We Use Information

All camera and photo processing happens locally on your device. We use the requested permissions solely to:

  • Display camera preview within the app interface
  • Capture photos and videos with the fill light effects you've configured
  • Save your captured content to your device's photo library (with your permission)
  • Allow you to select and import existing media from your photo library

Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is paramount to us:

  • All app functionality operates entirely offline
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • App settings are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • We cannot access your data remotely

Third-Party Services

Smart Light Assistant does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions completely independently on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect data, we comply with children's privacy regulations by design.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since the app doesn't collect personal data, policy changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.
